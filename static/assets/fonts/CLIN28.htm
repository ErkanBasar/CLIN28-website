VENUE

Conference will be held at

Meeting and Conference Centre Soeterbeeck

Elleboogstraat 2 - 25371 LL - Ravenstein

BY CAR

Ravenstein is located on the A50 Arnhem - Den Bosch, about twenty kilometers south of Nijmegen. On the A50, take both from Den Bosch and from Arnhem, Ravenstein exit (exit 17). From Den Bosch, turn left at the bottom of the exit and then again left towards Ravenstein. Pass under the A50 motorway and go straight on the roundabout.

From Arnhem: take exit 17, take 3 quarters of the roundabout and go straight on the second roundabout.

When you have passed under the railway viaduct, you can already see Soeterbeeck at your left side. At the next roundabout take the third exit (de Rijt) and then turn the first left (Elleboogstraat) again. Go through the gate to the parking lot of Soeterbeeck. 

ROUTENET

BY TRAIN

Both from 's-Hertogenbosch and from Nijmegen runs twice an hour a train to Ravenstein. For exact departure - and arrival of the train station Ravenstein, you can consult the planner of NS. The meeting and conference center Soeterbeeck is about a 10 minute walk from station Ravenstein and is accessible via the Stationspad, at the backside of the station.
